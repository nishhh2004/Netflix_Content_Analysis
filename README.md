# Netflix Content Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed the Netflix titles dataset to explore content trends,
country-wise distribution, rating patterns and yearly growth
of movies and TV shows on the platform.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Dataset
Netflix titles dataset containing 8807 titles with information
on type, country, rating, release year and date added.

## Key Insights
- Movies dominate Netflix with 70% of content vs 30% TV Shows
- Most common rating is TV-MA indicating mature audience focus
- United States produces the most content followed by India and UK
- Netflix grew rapidly from 2014 to 2019 with peak additions in 2019
- There is significant growth opportunity in regional TV Show content

## Recommendation
Netflix should invest more in TV Shows especially in regional
languages from India, Japan and South Korea to increase
audience engagement and watch time.
